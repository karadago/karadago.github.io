About Me

Welcome to my homepage.

I am a structural engineer working within the Department of Architecture, where I also serve as the Internship Coordinator. My work bridges the gap between complex structural engineering principles and architectural design, aiming to integrate robust construction materials and analytical methods into the built environment.

I am open for an assistant professorship to further my research and teaching in materials and structures.
